AboutPlacements

Our Graduates pursue Post Graduate and Research Studies in several institutions of repute within and outside the country. The Placement wing in the college is successful in providing placements to the out-going students through campus recruitment conducted by many organization of national and international repute. The placement officer notifies the names of the companies that visit for placements to the students through circulars. Our Placement Officer: M.PAVANI M.Tech(IM), Professor of Electronic Communication Engineering

Welcome



The Placement Cell of the Institute centrally handles all aspects of on-campus placements for the graduating students of all Dempartments.The Placement Cell is well equipped with excellent infrastructure to support each and every stage of the placement processes.The Placement staff members assist in arranging Pre-Placement Talks, Written Tests, Group Discussions, and Interviews etc.are made as per the requirements of the Organizations.
